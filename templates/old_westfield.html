The Mission

Old Westfield Photography is a company dedicated to servicing clients and professionals. Our cloud-platform establishes independent photographers with a myriad of online tools to succeed in the competitive field of photography. Our group of affiliated photographers deliver affordable photography by using talented students and newly established photographers. We provide our photographers with a network of potential clients to work for to build their business as a freelance photographer without the hassle of bothersome administrative tasks. With minimal transaction fees on services provided our affiliated photographers no longer need to handle billing, scheduling, advertising, and other mundane tasks that keep them from doing what they do best.

What we photograph

Through our cloud platform and extensive affiliated network of photographers Old Westfield Photography can deliver flexiblity and competitive prices to any perspective client. Regardless of the event feel confident in our ability to capture the moment. See our services page to get a full spectrum of our services and recent work.

our tools
