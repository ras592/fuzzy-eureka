A new beginning

Old Westfield Photography is a business dedicated to providing affordable quality photography,
while also helping amazing new photographers get their start.

The Mission

Old Westfield Photography is essentially a group that helps photographers get their feet wet in the business of photography.  Our goal is to provide very affordable photography by using talented students and self-taught photographers who can't afford the start-up costs or don't know how to start a business as a freelance photographer.  We are here to get them noticed. We provide the right tools to these photographers so they could one day start their own freelance photography business or at least build their portfolio and make some money.

What we photograph

By having extremely competitive prices we hope it opens the door to everything.  If it can be photographed we would love to take the picture, whether it's for a birthday, a sports event, a new portrait, stock pictures or even for commercial use.  See our services page to get a full spectrum of what we photograph and some of our recent work.

Services

our tools

We pride ourselves on our tools.  Our photographers get a website where they can showcase their work and run a blog, basically an online business card.  They also get their own online store for making it easy to sell pictures to clients, and lots of cloud storage for keeping backups of clients photos and so much more.
